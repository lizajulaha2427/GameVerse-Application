Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find games that are compatible with my device?
We provide compatibility details for each game. You can check if a game is available for your platform (PC, Xbox, PlayStation, etc.) on the game’s detail page.
Do I need a subscription to access the games?
Yes, we offer different subscription plans to access our game library. Each plan comes with various perks and access levels, which you can find on our subscription page.
Can I download games for offline play?
Currently, our games are available for online streaming only. However, we are working on a feature to allow offline downloads for certain subscription tiers.
How frequently are new games added?
New games are added monthly to keep our library fresh. Our subscribers receive early access to newly added titles as well.
Is there a limit to how many games I can add to my library?
No, you can add as many games as you like to your personal library. However, your ability to play multiple games simultaneously may depend on your subscription tier.
Are there age restrictions for certain games?
Yes, some games have age restrictions based on their content. Please check the game’s detail page for information on age ratings.
Can I share my account with others?
For security reasons, accounts are meant for individual use. Sharing accounts is not permitted under our terms of service.
